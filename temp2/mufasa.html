In ‘The Lion King,’ a Pitbull Puppy Has A Heartwarming Reaction To Mufasa’s Death.

Animals
September 10, 2021admin

Luna the pitbull, who is four months old, takes a break from playing with her toys to watch The Lion King with her parents, Tennessee residents Hannah and Josh Meyers, in this beautiful video from 2019.




Luna’s reaction is wonderful when Mufasa dies tragically in the film. She appears to be impacted by the tragedy in the same way that a human would, illustrating that dogs indeed have enormous hearts.

Anyone who has seen Disney’s animated masterpiece will remember how heartbreaking the sequence in which Simba’s father, Mufasa, dies is.




It doesn’t get any easier even if you’ve seen the movie several times. Luna, a four-month-old pit bull, was watching the movie with her owners, Josh and Hannah Myers, at the time of the video.

Despite the fact that Luna was initially distracted by her toys, the intensity of this scene caught her attention. Josh Meyer posted her reaction on Facebook and commented, “She basically became mute as she gazed upon the screen in sympathetic astonishment.”




“We are not deserving of dogs. She’s only four months old and already demonstrates empathy. I’m excited to witness her reaction to the new live-action Lion King!”




Even if she hadn’t been following the plot, she could appreciate the gravity of the scene and its significance. She lay down, seemingly in silent contemplation of how vivid this scene was as it lingered.

It should never be assumed that intelligent creatures like dogs don’t have emotions, and that these emotions aren’t limited to being glad when you arrive home or being angry when their bowl is empty.




Their ability to empathize is a big part of why they can bond so effectively with people. Although Luna speaks a language other than ours, the language of emotions is universal.

Luna’s narrative is filled with emotion as well. According to The Dodo, she was adopted from a litter of 12 babies born to a rescued mother.




Luna was adopted by Josh and Hannah after they fell in love with her. Blake’s goodwill provided Luna’s mother a second chance at life and assisted in the search for Luna a loving home where she could be treated well.
